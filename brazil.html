Meet Anita

She lives in Sao Paulo, Brazil and was one of the first people that made me gain a new perspective. She is the most bright, vivacious person I have ever known and her influence on me will always be withstanding.

Now she goes to Florida State University after her family moved from Brazil to Florida and got a greencard there. She literally became one of my first best friends and I will never forget her.
